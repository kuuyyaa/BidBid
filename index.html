Frequently Asked Questions

What is the scope of BidBid?+

Insurance, Uniforms, Increase Deposits Return Rates, Improve Currency Exchange Rates, Stationary, Safety Equipment, Pantry items, Facility Management Services, Any items your company purchases.

What is required to use BidBid?+

All that is required from your company is to invite the approved list of suppliers to participate in the live e-tendering or e–auction using BidBid Digital Platform and they can start bidding instantly.

Does this solution support managing subsidiaries and sister companies?+

Certainly, BidBid offers the capability to add all company subsidiaries. Moreover, it provides seamless Subsidiary Supervision & Management, offering a comprehensive view of procurement data across branches in different countries or different locations.
